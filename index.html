Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

To reset your level progression back to Level 1, go to the Game Settings from the main menu and select "Reset Progress". This cannot be undone.

Why isn't VoiceOver speaking during crashes?

During a high-speed crash, the game automatically plays a crash cinematic which briefly pauses VoiceOver. Once the crashed screen appears, VoiceOver resumes reading your options.

How can I pause the game?

You can tap anywhere on the top-right corner of the screen or press the Pause button (if playing with a supported controller) to immediately halt the race.
